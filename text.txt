Product-Level Analysis

Analyzed product performance at book level

Key metrics:

- Total revenue per book

- Total orders & quantity sold

- Average rating

- Repeat purchase rate

Identified:

- Best-selling books

- High-rated but low-volume products

- Products with strong repeat behavior

Category & Attribute Analysis

Sales breakdown by:

- Book category (multi-level hierarchy)

- Thickness type (short vs long books)

- Format (ebook vs physical)

Compared:

- Revenue vs volume

- Rating vs sales performance

Product Dashboard Features

Interactive filters:

- Category

- Format

- Price range

- Time period

Use cases:

- Identify products to promote

- Decide which categories to expand

- Support pricing & inventory decisions
